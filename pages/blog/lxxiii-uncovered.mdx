<h3 id="puzzle-1-1"></h3>

### Puzzle #1.1
The first puzzle begins with the follow clue:

<div className="flex justify-center">
<code>?v=azo5Gjj9qMw</code>
</div>

<br/>
<br/>

<Collapse title={<h4>Solution</h4>}>
Although not immediately obvious, we observe that this is the `videoID` of a YouTube video.
Take for example the link of the following youtube video:

<div className="flex justify-center">
<code>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ</code>
</div>

the portion requesting `?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ` is the video ID. Therefore, in our case the video link
will be given as follows:

<div className="flex justify-center">
<code>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=azo5Gjj9qMw</code>
</div>

</Collapse>
